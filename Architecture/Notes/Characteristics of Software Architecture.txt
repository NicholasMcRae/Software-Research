Characteristics of Software Architecture : Architects separate architecture characteristics into broad categories depending upon operation, 
rarely appearing requirements, structure etc. Below some important characteristics which are commonly considered are explained.

Operational Architecture Characteristics :
Availability
Performance
Reliability
Low fault tolerance
Scalability

Structural Architecture Characteristics :
Configurability
Extensibility
Supportability
Portability
Maintainability

Cross-Cutting Architecture Characteristics :
Accessibility
Security
Usability
Privacy
Feasibility
